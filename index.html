Growth Principles 🌱

What consistently works — stay in your own lane

🎭

Be Unapologetically You

Your accent, your humour, your bluntness — that IS the content. Never tone yourself down. People subscribe to people they feel connected to.

🪞

Real Life Over Highlight Reel

The messier and more honest, the more it resonates. Specific and real always outperforms polished and perfect.

🇯🇲

Your Culture Is Your Superpower

Patois, Jamaican food, cultural comparisons — your background is your biggest differentiator. Use it every video.

🎙️

Tell Stories, Not Just Updates

Build tension, add feeling, land a reaction. Start in the middle of the story. Make them feel it.

💬

Build Community, Not Just Views

Reply to comments with videos. Do Q&As. Make your audience feel like insiders, not just viewers.

🔁

Create Recurring Formats

Build formats your audience expects. Formats build habit. Habit builds subscribers.

🎵

Use Trending Audio Weekly

Check TikTok's trending audio every week — especially dancehall and afrobeats for your niche.

😂

Humour Makes Everything Land

Comedy makes hard truths easier to share. Your natural wit is a superpower — don't hold back.
