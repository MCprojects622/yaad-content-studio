🎯 Curiosity Hooks

Make them need to know what happens next. Best for food/reaction videos, trying new things, and story-based content.

1"I tried [thing] and was not prepared for what happened…"
2"Nobody talks about [topic] but I'm going to…"
3"Wait until you see the end of this…"
4"What happened next had me absolutely speechless…"
5"I found out something today and I had to share it…"
6"If you knew what I know right now, you'd be shocked too…"
7"I tried a horned melon for the first time and my reaction says everything…"
8"You have to see what happened in this week's vlog…"
💡 Best for: Food reactions, trying new things, story time, vlog teasers

😤 Relatable Hooks

Make them feel seen immediately. Best for rants, adulting content, work frustrations, and day-in-the-life videos.

1"Tell me why [thing] happened to me today…"
2"If you've ever dealt with [situation], this video is for you"
3"The audacity. I cannot. Let me explain…"
4"Am I wrong for being upset about this?"
5"The way I walked into work today and immediately regretted it…"
6"Nobody told me adulting would feel like this every single day…"
7"Why does this keep happening to me specifically…"
8"The way I be minding my business and then…"
💡 Best for: Rants, work content, adulting, day-in-the-life, frustrations

💬 Opinion Hooks

Spark debate and drive comments. Best for hot takes, unpopular opinions, and anything you feel strongly about.

1"Unpopular opinion: [statement] — fight me in the comments"
2"Hot take and I stand by every single word…"
3"I said what I said. [opinion]."
4"I'm probably going to get hate for this but…"
5"Everyone disagrees with me on this and I genuinely don't care…"
6"This is my truth and you can take it or leave it…"
7"Nobody agrees with me on this but here we go…"
8"I need to say this because nobody else will…"
💡 Best for: Hot takes, opinion pieces, responding to trends, unpopular views

😂 Comedy Hooks

Stop the scroll with humour. Best for funny moments, chaos content, and anything where your personality is the whole point.

1"God was really testing me today…"
2"I was minding my business when…"
3"This is not what I planned but here we are…"
4"I cannot make this up, this actually happened to me today…"
5"Jamaican chaos for your Thursday…"
6Start mid-laugh or mid-reaction — no words needed at all
7"It's giving chaos and I am here for it…"
8"Story time because this had me absolutely dead…"
💡 Best for: Funny moments, chaos days, story time, personality-first content

Be Unapologetically You

Your accent, your humour, your bluntness — that IS the content. Never tone yourself down. People subscribe to people they feel connected to.

🪞

Real Life Over Highlight Reel

The messier and more honest, the more it resonates. Specific and real always outperforms polished and perfect.

🇯🇲

Your Culture Is Your Superpower

Patois, Jamaican food, cultural comparisons — your background is your biggest differentiator. Use it every video.

🎙️

Tell Stories, Not Just Updates

Build tension, add feeling, land a reaction. Start in the middle of the story. Make them feel it.

💬

Build Community, Not Just Views

Reply to comments with videos. Do Q&As. Make your audience feel like insiders, not just viewers.

🔁

Create Recurring Formats

Build formats your audience expects. Formats build habit. Habit builds subscribers.

🎵

Use Trending Audio Weekly

Check TikTok's trending audio every week — especially dancehall and afrobeats for your niche.

😂

Humour Makes Everything Land

Comedy makes hard truths easier to share. Your natural wit is a superpower — don't hold back.

⏰

Post Consistently

Once a week on YouTube, daily on TikTok/Reels when starting. Consistency beats frequency every time. Pick a schedule you can actually keep.

📊

Check Analytics Weekly

Look at watch time and click-through rate above everything else. Double down on what works. The data will tell you what your audience loves.
